FAQ


What is the purpose of BuzzFood?

The purpose of BuzzFood is to offer healthy and trending recipes, as well as recommended places to eat healthy food. BuzzFood also aims to educate young adults on the benefits and ways to maintain a healthy diet, by supplying information and tools for users to estimate their dietary health.

Why is it important to maintain a healthy diet?

Having a healthy diet ensures that your body gets all the nutrition it needs and runs properly. Eating unhealthily can cause a wide variety of health risks that are associated with obesity, having a lack lack of nutrients or having excess salt or sugar in the body.

How can I get more information on my dietary health?

To get more information on your dietary health, visit a doctor so they can answer your specific questions and concerns, as well as get a professional’s opinion on your dietary habits. If you are looking for general information on how everyone can maintain a healthy diet, there are many websites that offer good recommendations, such as https://www.eatforhealth.gov.au/.

How accurate is the BMI calculator?

The BMI calculator which we offer on our site itself is very accurate when it comes to the numerical value of your BMI. However BMI is not a very accurate scale for measuring whether or not a person is obese or eating healthily. This is largely caused by the fact that it only takes into account a person’s height and weight. Different bodies have different muscle mass and bone density among other things, which cannot be accounted for when calculating at home.
