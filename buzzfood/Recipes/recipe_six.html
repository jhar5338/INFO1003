Steamed Asian greens with honey soy sauce

  • Overview
    Prep Time Cook Time Ingredients Serves
    10 Minutes 6 Minutes 6 4
  • Ingredients
    • tablespoons salt-reduced soy sauce
    • 1 tablespoon honey
    • 2cm piece fresh ginger, peeled, finely grated
    • 1 bunch baby choy sum, trimmed
    • 1 bunch broccolini, trimmed
    • 1/2 teaspoon sesame seeds, toasted
  • Method
    1. 1. Combine soy sauce, honey and ginger in a jug. Separate leaves from choy sum stems.
    2. 2. Place a large steamer over a wok or large saucepan of simmering water. Place choy sum stems and broccolini in steamer. Cook, covered, for 3 to 4 minutes or until just tender. Add choy sum leaves. Cook, covered, for 1 to 2 minutes or until wilted. Transfer to a bowl. Add sauce mixture. Toss to combine. Sprinkle with sesame seeds. Serve.
